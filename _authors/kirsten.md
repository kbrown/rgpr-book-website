Kirsten Widner is Assistant Professor of Political Science at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville. She holds a PhD in Political Science from Emory University (2020) and a JD from the University of San Diego (2007). Her research focuses on how laws and policies that affect marginalized groups are made.
